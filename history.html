Our History

Albert Heijn Stokker Through the Years

  • 1887

    Albert Heijn opens his first grocery store in Oostzaan, Netherlands.

  • 1920

    Albert Heijn becomes a family-run chain, expanding across the country.

  • 1984

    First Albert Heijn in Burenbarg, in Burenburg, opened by Daniel Gray.

  • 1987

    AH Gray limited. becomes a seperate franchise from Albert Heijn Burenburg, with 16 stores.

  • 1989

    First self-checkout tills in AH North Stokker (Gray).

  • 1999

    2 AH Grays turn into an Albert Heijn ToGo.

  • 2000

    Launch of the AH Bonus program, offering personalized discounts.

  • 2016

    AH Gray limited. rebrands to AH Stokker LTD.

  • 2017

    FreshFR formula transforms Albert Heijn Stokker

  • 2024

    The AH Stokker Group LTD achieves sustainability milestones and launches a modern website. We also have 12 stores in Burenburg, of which 9 normal stores, 2 ToGo's and 1 XL.

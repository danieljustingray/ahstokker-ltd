Teamleader (12–40 hours)

Tasks Included:

  • Supervising and assisting team members with restocking shelves according to store guidelines.
  • Coordinating the setup of promotional displays and ensuring compliance with store standards.
  • Inspecting and organizing deliveries, verifying seals, and addressing any discrepancies.
  • Monitoring inventory levels, placing replenishment orders, and managing stock rotation.
  • Reporting damages or operational issues directly to management.
  • Ensuring that the storage area remains organized, clean, and compliant with safety regulations.
  • Leading efforts in sorting packaging materials for recycling or disposal.
  • Providing guidance to team members and assisting customers as needed.
  • Organizing the collection of baskets and shopping carts and maintaining the cleanliness of store premises.
  • Managing the day-to-day flow of operations and delegating tasks effectively.
  • Completing other leadership duties as required by the store manager.

Requirements:

  • You are at least 16 years old.
  • You have a secondary school diploma (vmbo kader, mavo, havo 3 or vwo 3).
  • You have prior experience in retail or team management (pre).
  • You live within a 35 km radius of the store

What’s in it for you?

  • A great salary based on scale C (see salary table).
  • Reimbursement for travel costs beyond 5 km.
  • A clean, supportive, and enjoyable work environment.
  • Opportunities for career growth and leadership training within AH Stokker.
